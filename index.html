Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Online PGDM program approved by AICTE?
Yes, the Online Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) offered by Jaipuria Institute of Management is fully approved by AICTE and recognized by UGC-DEB.
Do I need to visit the campus for examinations?
No, the entire program including learning, assignments, and semester-end examinations is conducted 100% online through an AI-proctored secure platform.
What is the eligibility criteria?
Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree (10+2+3 or 4) in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for reserved categories) from a recognized university. Work experience is preferred but not mandatory.
Will it say 'Online' on my degree/diploma certificate?
As per UGC guidelines, the mode of delivery (Online) will be mentioned on the final diploma certificate, however, the degree holds equivalence to a regular MBA program as recognized by AIU.
