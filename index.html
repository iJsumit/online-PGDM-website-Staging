About Jaipuria Institute of Management

Established in 2004, Jaipuria Institute of Management, Noida is a leading B-School in India known for its strong academic foundation and deep industry integration. In 2023, the institute introduced its AICTE-approved Online PGDM, enrolling 200+ students within the first year - reflecting its growing relevance among working professionals and aspiring managers.

The programme is designed to deliver experiential learning, industry exposure, and structured mentorship that support holistic career growth. Backed by strong corporate connections, innovative pedagogy, and a modern campus ecosystem, Jaipuria Noida continues to develop future-ready business leaders equipped to succeed in a rapidly evolving, AI-driven business environment.
