Overview

ABOUT JAIPURIA INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT, NOIDA

Established in 2004, Jaipuria Institute of Management, Noida is a leading B-School in India known for its strong academic foundation and deep industry integration. In 2023, the institute introduced its AICTE-approved Online PGDM, enrolling 200 plus students within the first year, reflecting its growing relevance among working professionals and aspiring managers.

The programme is designed to deliver experiential learning, industry exposure, and structured mentorship that support holistic career growth. Backed by strong corporate connections, innovative pedagogy, and a modern campus ecosystem, Jaipuria Noida continues to develop future-ready business leaders equipped to succeed in a rapidly evolving, AI-driven business environment while also offering its flagship full-time PGDM and a Doctoral-Level Fellow Programme.

FAQs

Is the Online PGDM approved and recognized?

Yes, the programme is AICTE-approved and recognized, ensuring credibility and industry acceptance across India.

Can I pursue this programme while working full-time?

Yes, the programme is specifically designed for working professionals with weekend classes and flexible learning options.

What is the duration of the Online PGDM?

The programme typically spans 2 years, divided into multiple semesters.

Are live classes mandatory to attend?

Live classes are recommended for better learning, but recordings are available for flexibility if you miss a session.

What specialisations are offered in the programme?

The programme offers new-age specialisations such as AI and Business Analytics, Digital and E-Commerce, Product Management, and Entrepreneurship.

How are exams and assessments conducted?

Assessments are conducted online through quizzes, assignments, projects, and term-end examinations with flexible scheduling.

Is there any placement or career support?

Yes, the programme offers continuous career support including resume building, interview preparation, and placement assistance.

Will I get networking opportunities in an online programme?

Yes, you will interact with peers, faculty, and industry experts through live sessions, group projects, and campus immersion.

What is the eligibility criteria for admission?

Candidates must have a minimum 3-year bachelor’s degree with at least 50% marks from a recognized university.

How do I apply for the programme?

You can apply online by filling out the application form, submitting required documents, and completing the admission process.
